Lumberjack Tree Service of Dallas, TX

Lumberjack Tree Service in Dallas, TX has provided tree care services for the people of Dallas and Ft. Worth for over 18 years. We are proud to offer a full range of tree services. These include tree removal, tree pruning or trimming, hedge trimming, tree planting, chipping and tree fertilization. Our experienced tree crews provide great onsite customer service to exceed your expectation. Just as no two trees are the same, we know that no two projects are the same and we treat each as such. We understand that the most important service we can provide is satisfaction and peace of mind.
The owner Derek Hebert has been providing tree services to the Dallas metroplex for years. He understands the value of knowing his customers personally and making sure they can always speak with him directly if there are ever any questions or concerns. We work harder than anyone to make sure our customers are completely satisfied. Lumberjack Tree Service serves Dallas, Plano, Garland, McKinney, Carrollton, Lewsiville, Flower Mound, Coppell, Richardson, Rowlett, Rockwall, Mesquite, and other surrounding cities in the Dallas metroplex.

We are a Christian-owned tree company and committed to providing you the highest quality tree service in Dallas.
Past experience is often the best way to gauge what a tree company can do for you. Over the years, we have developed an impressive body of work that speaks for itself. In addition, the relationships we have made with our customers is second to none. As a result, not only do we leave on a positive note, but we keep coming back for your repeat business. Lumberjack Tree Service will provide references from customers who would be more than happy to let you know about the quality service you can expect from us if requested.

Well-Equipped to Handle All Tree Services
At Lumberjack Tree Services, we also use modern, well-kept equipment. Why should that matter to you? There are two main reasons. The first is that we ensure that the health of your trees is a priority. Usually dull, old equipment can lead to incomplete or damaging cuts to the trees causing browning or leaving them open to infection or infestation. Also, the better the equipment the faster the service. This saves customers time and money.

Tree Services

Since many people don’t deal with these issues on a regular basis, they may not even know where to begin. Thankfully, with our years of experience and know-how, we can help you resolve those problems quickly and affordably. We’ll make it look like it never even happened.
Since we offer a wide variety of services, we invite you to become acquainted with what it is exactly that we can do for you.
